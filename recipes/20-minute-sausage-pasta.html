20-Minute Sausage Pasta

Description

Only 20-minutes, you'll have a delectable pasta meal suitable for the family! Can even use chicken sausage if you're looking for a different vibe.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces penne pasta
  • 2 italian chicken sausages, such as Aidell's
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, finely minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 12 ounces marinara sauce
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil, add pasta, and cook until tender with a bite, 9 to 11 minutes, or according to package directions. Reserve about 1/4 cup pasta water; drain.
  2. Meanwhile, slice chicken sausages in half, then slice each half into small bite-sized pieces.
  3. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat and add sausage. Cook and stir until sausage is lightly browned, about 3 minutes. Scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet.
  4. Add butter and garlic to the skillet; cook until garlic is fragrant, about 1 minute.
  5. Add marinara sauce and cream to the skillet. Cook until combined and completely warmed through; stir in pasta. If sauce is too thick, thin with a little pasta water.
  6. Remove from heat and add in as much Parmesan as you like. Serve in deep bowls with bread to scoop up the extra sauce.
