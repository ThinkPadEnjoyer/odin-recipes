Bacon Wrapped Cheese Crackers

Description

Perfectly cooked bacon wrapped crackers with cheese! Some assembly required, and some baking later.. You now have a delicious low calorie snack.

Ingredients

  • 48 buttery round crackers, such as Ritz crackers
  • 1 (8 ounce) square block white Cheddar cheese, such as Cracker Barrell, cut into 24 slices
  • 2 jalapenos, sliced
  • 12 slices bacon, cut in half crosswise
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F(150 Degrees C). Place half the crackers on a parchment or foil lined rimmed baking sheet.
  2. Top each cracker with a slice of cheese and a slice of jalapeno. Place another cracker on top of each one
  3. Wrap each cracker sandwich with a piece of bacon and place, seam side down on the baking sheet. Top each evenly with brown sugar, lightly pressing it into the bacon.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until brown and caramelized, 1 hour to 1 hour 20 minutes. Let cool slightly before serving.
